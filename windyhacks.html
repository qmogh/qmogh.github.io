My Role in Chicago's High School Hackathon

I spent six months with some of my closest friends organizing Windy City Hacks, a 24 hour hackathon for students all over the midwest to program games and apps. Hackers had access to mentors and workshops and the event was entirely free.

This incredible event was June 22nd-23rd in one of the best weekends of my life. My role on the team was sponsorship and hacker experience, and as a whole our event raised $14k and had 320 (160 attended) students sign up.
